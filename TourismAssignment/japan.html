About Japan

Japan is an island nation located in East Asia. It is known for its rich culture, advanced technology, delicious cuisine, and beautiful natural landscapes. From modern cities like Tokyo to peaceful temples and mountains, Japan offers a unique travel experience.

Map of Japan

Japan is made up of four main islands: Honshu, Hokkaido, Kyushu, and Shikoku. It is located in the Pacific Ocean and is close to countries such as China and South Korea.
