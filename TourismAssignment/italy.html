About Italy

Italy is a country located in Southern Europe. It is known for its rich history, world-famous art, delicious food, and beautiful landscapes. From ancient ruins to modern cities, Italy offers something for every traveler.

Map of Italy

Italy is shaped like a boot and extends into the Mediterranean Sea. It shares borders with France, Switzerland, Austria, and Slovenia. Major cities include Rome, Milan, Venice, and Florence.

The Colosseum

The Colosseum is one of the most famous landmarks in Italy. Located in Rome, it was built nearly 2,000 years ago and was used for gladiator contests and public events. Today, millions of tourists visit the Colosseum each year to explore its history and impressive architecture.
